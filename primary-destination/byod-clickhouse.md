# BYOD ClickHouse

**BYOD ClickHouse** lets you connect QUANTI: to your own ClickHouse instance. Your data is loaded directly into your ClickHouse database, enabling high-performance analytical queries at scale.

This option is ideal if your organization relies on ClickHouse for its speed and efficiency on large volumes of data.

BYOD ClickHouse is available on the **Enterprise** plan only.
