# BYOD BigQuery

**BYOD BigQuery** (Bring Your Own Data Warehouse) lets you connect QUANTI: to your own Google BigQuery instance. Your data is loaded directly into your GCP project, giving you full ownership and control over your data warehouse.

This option is ideal if you already have a GCP infrastructure, want to keep your data within your own cloud environment, or need to consolidate QUANTI: data alongside existing datasets.

BYOD BigQuery is available on all plans.
