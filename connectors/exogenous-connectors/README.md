# Exogenous connectors

Exogenous connectors bring in external, non-marketing data that provides context to explain variations in your campaign performance. They are designed to enrich your data warehouse with signals like seasonality, weather conditions, and audience demographics — essential inputs for marketing mix modeling (MMM) and performance analysis.
