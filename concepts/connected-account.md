# Connected Account

A **Connected Account** is an advertising or analytics account from an external platform that you have connected to QUANTI: — for example, a specific Meta Ads ad account, a Google Ads account, or a LinkedIn Ads account.

A Connected Account always belongs to a single [Data Source](data-source.md). You can connect multiple accounts from the same Data Source — for instance, two different Meta Ads ad accounts for two different markets.

Connected Accounts are the **unit of billing** in QUANTI:. The number you can activate depends on your plan:

| Plan | Connected Accounts |
|---|---|
| Free | 1 |
| Pro | 5 |
| Growth | 15 |
| Expert | 50 |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |
